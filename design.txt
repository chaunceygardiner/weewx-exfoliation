Design of the forecast display

  The forecast page displays a vertical column of data from now into the
  future, with the oldest date first.  Each date line can be expanded to
  show the 3- or 6-hour detailed forecast for that day.  Each date line
  contains aggregate values for those data that vary over the course of
  the day.
